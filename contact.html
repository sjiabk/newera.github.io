Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions below

What types of projects do you handle?

CDS Contractors specializes in a wide range of construction projects, including commercial buildings, residential homes, renovations, remodeling, and custom builds. Our experienced team can handle projects of various sizes, from small residential renovations to large commercial developments.

How do I request a quote for my project?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form on this page, calling our main office, or sending an email to info@cdscontractors.com. Please provide as much detail about your project as possible to help us give you an accurate estimate. Our team will get back to you within 1-2 business days to discuss your project requirements.

What geographic areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Pacific Northwest region with offices in Seattle, Portland, and San Francisco. Our service areas extend to surrounding communities within approximately a 100-mile radius of each office. For larger projects, we may consider locations outside these areas. Please contact us to discuss your specific location.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on the scope, complexity, and size of the work. A small renovation might take a few weeks, while a large commercial building could take many months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a realistic timeline based on your specific project requirements, taking into account factors like permitting, material procurement, and construction schedules.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, as part of our comprehensive service, we handle all necessary permits and approvals for your project. Our team is experienced in navigating local building codes, zoning regulations, and approval processes. We'll take care of the paperwork and coordination with local authorities to ensure your project meets all legal requirements.
